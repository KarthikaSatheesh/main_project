Local Government Institution, Idukki District, Kerala






About Chakkupallam


According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Chakkupallam village is 628082. Chakkupallam village is located in Udumbanchola Tehsil of Idukki district in Kerala, India. It is situated 34km away from sub-district headquarter Nedumkandam and 55km away from district headquarter Painavu. As per 2009 stats, Chakkupallam village is also a gram panchayat. The total geographical area of village is 2833 hectares. Chakkupallam has a total population of 12,609 peoples. There are about 3,429 houses in Chakkupallam village. As per 2019 stats, Chakkupallam villages comes under Peerumade assembly & Idukki parliamentary constituency. Thodupuzha is nearest town to Chakkupallam which is approximately 103km away. Chakkupallam is a Gram (village) Panchayat in Idukki district in the Indian state of Kerala. The administrative capital(Panchayat Office) and village center of Chakkupallam Gram(Village) Panchayat is Anakkara, located on Kumily-Munnar state highway. The town has a large church, built in 2000.
